Please draw a route from A to B and press "Submit".

To draw a route on the map, click on the road segments to form a path from A to B. As you click on a segment of the road, it will be shown as yellow. To change your route at any time, press the “Delete route” button. This will erase your entire route and enable you to start again. To remove a selected segment of the route, click on the route a second time and you will see it will change from yellow to unselected.
